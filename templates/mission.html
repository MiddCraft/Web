WELCOME TO MIDDCRAFT
What is MiddCraft?
MiddCraft is a collaborative project run by Middlebury students with the goal of creating a virtual map of Middlebury College in Minecraft.

Mission
Our mission is to create a space for the Middlebury community to come together and rebuild our beautiful campus in a time of social distancing. No matter who you are or where you come from, welcome!
