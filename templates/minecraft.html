WHAT IS MINECRAFT?
Minecraft is a game where players can build three-dimensional worlds using blocks. One of the greatest things about Minecraft is its accessibility; it does not require a fancy gaming computer. Anyone with a Mac, Windows, or Linux PC can play!

Don't have Minecraft? It's as easy as going to minecraft.org and creating an account! Once you have an account, Minecraft costs $27 to own.
