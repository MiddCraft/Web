GET INVOLVED
Are you a current or former student, faculty or staff member of Middlebury? You can register with us and join our growing community.

Steps to Join the Server

  • Make sure you are logged in to your Middlebury email on your web browser
  • Click this link and fill out a google form. The form will ask for your minecraft username and Middlebury email.
  • Wait for an email confirmation that you have been promoted to the appropriate rank. We typically respond within 24 hours.
  • Log in to Minecraft and enjoy your new chat and build privileges!

We use a ranking system that represents the different members of our Middlebury community. The default rank is “Friend,” which has no chat or build privileges. All of the ranks listed below have chat and build privileges.

Student : For current students of Middlebury College.
Alum : For alumni of Middlebury College.
Staff : For current staff of Middlebury College.
Faculty : For current faculty of Middlebury College.
Administrative : For current administrative staff of Middlebury College.

Builder : For members of our core team of dedicated builders.
Admin : For server creators/admins.
